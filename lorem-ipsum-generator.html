Why designers still use Lorem Ipsum

Lorem Ipsum is scrambled Latin, deliberately meaningless so it doesn't distract from evaluating layout, typography, and spacing — real sentences pull a reviewer's attention to the words instead of the design. It's been the standard placeholder text in publishing and design since the 1960s, and remains useful today for mockups, wireframes, CMS templates, and print layouts before final copy is ready.
