Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What this site does

Free Online Tools provides browser-based utilities (JSON formatting, password/QR/UUID generation, image resizing, PDF merging, and similar tools). All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript — the text, images, or files you use with these tools are not uploaded to or stored on any server we operate. We have no accounts, no database, and no way to see what you type into these tools.

Advertising and cookies

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertising. Google and its advertising partners may use cookies, device identifiers, or similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or opt out of third-party vendor use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info.

Analytics

We may use standard web analytics (such as Google Analytics or GitHub Pages' built-in traffic logs) to understand aggregate traffic to the site — for example, how many people visit and which pages are popular. This data is anonymized/aggregated and is not linked to the content you enter into any tool.

Third-party services

Some tools load small third-party JavaScript libraries from a CDN (for example, QR code and PDF libraries) to function. These libraries run in your browser and do not receive the content you process with the tools.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from them.
