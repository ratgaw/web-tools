Split / Extract PDF Pages

Two ways to split a PDF

Extract page range pulls out just the pages you specify — enter something like 1-3,5,8 to grab pages 1 through 3 plus pages 5 and 8 — and produces one new PDF containing only those pages, in order. Useful for pulling a chapter, a signed page, or a relevant section out of a longer document.

Split into individual pages breaks every page of the document into its own single-page PDF, each with its own download link — handy when you need to hand off, archive, or re-scan pages one at a time.

Both modes run through pdf-lib entirely in your browser, so the original document never leaves your device.
